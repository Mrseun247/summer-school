Frequently asked questions

Still have questions? Email us at hello@codespark.dev

Does my child need a laptop?
Yes — each student needs their own laptop running Windows, macOS, or ChromeOS. We'll send a setup guide before camp starts. Tablets and phones won't work for most tracks.
What if my child has never coded before?
That's exactly who we designed this for. The Web Dev and Game Dev tracks start from zero. Our coaches are trained to meet students exactly where they are.
Is the programme in-person or online?
CodeMind Summer 2026 runs online.
What is the student-to-coach ratio?
We cap each track at 7 students with 1 coach, giving a 7:1 ratio. This is intentional — everyone gets real attention.
Can siblings register for different tracks?
Absolutely. Submit a separate registration for each child. Mention in the notes that they're siblings.
